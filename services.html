OUR SEVICES

Our Services

Spanning several decades and thousands of product certifications, our project managers and support engineers have developed fast and efficient methods and strategies to achieve the compliance outcomes you need.

From pre-compliance testing and assistance with standards interpretations in order to achieve conformance for EMC, safety, Interworking and NEBS, we're able to follow through to achieve rapid 1st tier country certifications such as North America, CE marking for Europe and Japanese approvals from JATE, Telec and Denan

Our offices and agents can then support your product rollout throughout Asia, South America and the Middle East, holding certifications on your behalf where necessary.

Where testing in Government labs is necessary, our local offices and agents arrange importation of samples and the necessary customs clearance, liaise with the lab as to the minimum suite of tests required, hand carry test samples to the lab and where necessary, provide witness testing. For complex equipment that requires specific engineering support, we accompany a manufacturer's support engineer to the lab, provide necessary translations and work to solve any non-compliance issues that may arise.

Global Approvals

"Market Access" encompasses our pre-testing, testing and approvals services, while incorporating our in-country knowledge to assist with selection of channel partners and customs clearance.

We provide an unbiased opinion as to prospective channel partners and can arrange or facilitate initial meetings.

As an example, we are able to compile a list of companies capable of distributing your products within a specific country, forward their company profiles including our assessment of their weaknesses and strengths, assist with short listing candidates and arrange meetings between both parties. We also provide translation or interpreter services where required.

This service is provided at a fixed hourly rate and free of back room commissions to ensure unbiased selection of the right company.

Consulting

Our extensive global network provides more than just product approvals.

For new or emerging technologies, an approvals path may not exist. We provide in-country lobbying to Governments, Regulators and Carriers to achieve desired outcomes. Wireless and spectrum management issues are also encompassed. We leverage off the experience of our local agents to ensure dedicated in-country support for your activities.

As a client utilizing our services, you're welcome to request info relating to specific markets or changes to legislation that will impact on the technology that your company promotes.

We also provide support by connecting manufacturers with in-country channel partners experienced in product distribution and marketing. This is particularly important in Asia, where the right partner is critical to success. Refer to our "market access" page for more info.

Pre Testing

WWe have many years of expertise when evaluating products against global telecom, EMC and safety standards. This experience stretches to telecom testing services and telecom equipment certification. Where 3rd party testing is accepted and has not yet been performed, we are able to provide quotations from various, cost competitive partner test labs.

Likewise, where 3rd partly results are not accepted (CMC/SIRIM for Malaysia, NALTE and SRRC for China as examples), we are able to perform preliminary evaluations of your product to ensure the in-country test process is smooth and trouble free.

In-House Test and pre-telecom testing services are offered for:

Safety Standards IEC/UL60950, IEC 60601-1, IEC 60065, IEC 60335 and sub sections, including CB Reports and Certs.

EMC emission and immunity standards such as EN55011, EN55012, EN55013, EN55014-1, EN55015, EN55022, EN55024, FCC Part 15, EN50082-1 & -2.
